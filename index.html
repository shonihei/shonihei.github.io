Huddle

Large group meeting made simple!

Finding meeting times for large groups are difficult. When I was taking my business plan course in college, we hopped between 3 or 4 online tools before giving up and manually finding meeting times that accounted for everyone's schedule. By using users' Google Calendar data, Huddle automatically finds optimal meeting times given some duration.
